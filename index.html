About Me

Hello and welcome to my personal page. This page started as a project for a Web Development class but hopefully will evolve into a fully functional, professional portfolio page bu the time I'm done with it. I'm currently attending classes for my last semester Cal State East Bay University pursuing a B.S. in Computer Science. I am set to graduate in December 2025. For the last 10 years I have worked as Stagehand and Stage Rigger for IATSE Local 16 in San Francisco. During my career I have had the opportunity to work with Broadway theatrical productions, countless live concert events and music festivals, the SF Symphony, and several films and television shows. I have enjoyed my time as a stagehand and will forever look back at it fondly as some of the best years of my adult life. That being said, I made a decision earlier this year to exit the entertainment industry to pursue a career in the technology sector. I have have always been passionate about computers and technology and I am looking forward to transitioning from a hobbyist to a technology professional.
