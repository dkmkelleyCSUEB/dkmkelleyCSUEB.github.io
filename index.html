About Me

Hello and welcome to my personal page. This page started as a project for a Web Development class but hopefully will evolve into a fully functional, professional portfolio page by the time I'm done with it. I'm currently finishing my last semester at Cal State East Bay University pursuing a B.S. in Computer Science. I am set to graduate in December 2025. For the last 10 years I have worked as a Stagehand and Stage Rigger for IATSE Local 16 in San Francisco. During my career I have had the opportunity to work with touring Broadway theatrical productions, countless live concert events and music festivals, the SF Symphony, trade shows, product launches, conferences and several films and television shows. I have enjoyed my time as a stagehand and will forever look back at it fondly as some of the best years of my working adult life. That being said, I made a decision earlier this year to exit the entertainment industry to pursue a career in the technology sector. I have have always been passionate about computers and technology and I am looking forward to transitioning from a hobbyist to a technology professional.I am currently seeking positions in the following fields: software development, software QA, systems administration, AI/ML, and information security.
